All Studies Gene List Overlap

Goal

This notebook combines all extracted gene lists from notebooks in ../data-extraction/ (excluding index.qmd) into one table and one heatmap.

  • Rows represent genes.
  • Columns represent individual extracted lists.
  • TRUE (or a colored tile) means that gene appears in that list.

This gives a quick visual summary of overlap and differences across studies.

FerrDB Coverage by Study Count

For each number of studies in which a gene was identified, the bar plot below shows what fraction of those genes are already catalogued in FerrDB v3. A rising trend would indicate that genes replicated across more studies are progressively more likely to be known ferroptosis modulators.

Gene List Hit Counts per Gene

The table below counts how many individual gene lists (across all studies and conditions) contain each gene. Unlike the section above, no deduplication by study is applied: a gene appearing in four lists from the same study contributes 4 to its count. This is useful when lists within a study are not independent — for example, when the same screen is thresholded at multiple conditions — but the totals should be interpreted with that caveat in mind.
